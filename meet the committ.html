Priya's Journey: From Beneficiary to Leader

In 2001, 8-year-old Priya was found selling flowers at a busy intersection by our founder Ssaka Daglous. Her parents, struggling to make ends meet, couldn't afford to send her to school. This encounter became the catalyst for our "Education for All" initiative.

With Kalayilayi's support, Priya not only completed her education but excelled academically. She became the first in her family to attend university, earning a degree in Social Work.

"Kalayilayi didn't just give me an education - they gave me a family and a purpose. Now I get to help other children discover their potential, just as they helped me."

Today, Priya serves as our Regional Education Coordinator, overseeing programs that support over 500 children annually. Her office wall displays her first flower basket - a reminder of how far compassion can take us.

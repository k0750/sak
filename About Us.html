Our Journey of Impact

In 2010, we began with a simple belief: every community deserves access to sustainable solutions. What started as a small initiative to provide clean water in one village has grown into a global movement spanning 2 districtic and one county.

Our mission remains focused on creating lasting change through education, healthcare, and economic empowerment. To date, we've impacted over 500,000 lives, but our work is far from done.

  • Compassion: We meet communities where they are, listening to their unique needs.
  • Integrity: Every dollar donated is accounted for with full transparency.
  • Sustainability: We build programs that continue to thrive long after our initial involvement.

Our Legacy

For generations, Kalayilayi & Son has stood as a beacon of excellence, integrity, and innovation. Rooted in tradition yet driven by progress, we have built a reputation for delivering unparalleled quality and trust in every endeavor.

Priya's Journey

Priya Kalayilayi, Director of Operations

From a young age, Priya Kalayilayi was drawn to the family business, often spending weekends observing her father's meticulous approach to client relationships. After earning her MBA from the University of Chicago, she returned home with a mission: to bridge tradition with innovation.

Under her leadership, Kalayilayi & Son adopted sustainable practices, expanded into new markets, and launched mentorship programs for women in the industry.

"What excites me isn't just profit—it's the people behind every project. When we invest in our team and community, success follows naturally."

How Child Sponsorship Works

Child sponsorship helps us connect you with a child you believe in. When you sponsor a child, you are helping to break the cycle of poverty. Not only do you transform the life of a child, your generosity also extends to the entire community around them.

Your sponsorship support will provide access to basic necessities such as food, clean water, education, and health care. Because of our child-focused community approach, for every child you sponsor, four more children benefit too.

Child sponsorship is available to help children in many communities among 35 countries. With your belief, we can transform the life of a child and their community for good.

EDUCATION

Access to education, classroom repairs, school supplies and training for teachers and parents provide a child with opportunities for a better future.

SAFE WATER

Access to safe water through well-drilling, pumps and training in hygiene and sanitation decreases child mortality and frees up time for education instead of water collection.

NUTRITIOUS FOOD

Going beyond food distribution to a focus on creating sustainable food supplies through agricultural programs and nutritional training ensures that children are well-nourished and healthy.

HEALTHCARE

Good health is the foundation of a child’s life. Access to essential services, vaccinations, medical clinics and training in disease prevention provide stronger health for children and their communities.

CHILD PROTECTION & PARTICIPATION

We advocate for child rights and encourage children to speak up against abuse and injustice. We also work with local leaders to strengthen their skills and encourage networking with government groups.

ECONOMIC EMPOWERMENT

Microloans, business training and various trades and skills training enable families to provide for their children’s needs, helping them to build a better future.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How much does it cost to sponsor a child? +
    Sponsoring a child through Kalayilayi and Sons costs $35 per month, which provides essential support for education, health, and community development.
    How does child sponsorship work? +
    Child sponsorship connects you with a child in need. Your contributions help provide them with life-changing benefits like education, healthcare, and better living conditions.
    What does child sponsorship do for the child? +
    Your sponsorship ensures that the child receives access to basic needs, education, and health services, empowering them to build a brighter future.
    Will my sponsorship contributions go directly to my sponsored children or their family? +
    Contributions are pooled to fund programs that benefit your sponsored child and their entire community, ensuring long-term impact and development.
    Is the money I give to Kalayilayi and Sons handled responsibly? +
    Yes, Kalayilayi and Sons ensures that every dollar is allocated effectively and transparently to benefit children and their communities.
    How can I track my sponsored child's progress? +
    You will receive regular updates, including letters and photos, along with reports on community development projects.
    How are children selected for sponsorship? +
    Children are selected based on their family’s income, health conditions, and the level of support available in their community.
    How long will I sponsor my child? +
    Sponsorship typically lasts until the child completes their education or becomes self-reliant. You may also choose to continue supporting other children.
    Can I adopt or bring a sponsored child to my country? +
    Child sponsorship is not adoption. It is a program designed to support children in their local communities without removing them from their cultural environment.
    Will my monthly child sponsorship donation ever increase? +
    Kalayilayi and Sons strives to keep costs stable, but adjustments may occur to account for inflation or expanded services.

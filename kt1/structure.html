About City Warriors FC

Our Football Legacy

Founded in 2010 under the Kalayilayi and Sons sports initiative, City Warriors FC has grown from a local community team to a competitive force in regional football. We pride ourselves on developing young talent while maintaining strong community values.

Our home ground, Warrior Arena, located in the heart of the city, has witnessed countless memorable matches and has become a fortress for our team.

The club operates under three core principles: Passion, Discipline, and Community.
